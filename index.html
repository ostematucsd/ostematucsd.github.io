Our Mission

At oSTEM we are dedicated to fostering a supportive and inclusive community for LGBTQ+ individuals in STEM. Through mentorship, professional development, and social events, we empower students to thrive academically, professionally, and personally. Join us in building a future where diversety drives innovation!

In accordance with applicable Fedral and State law and University policy, Out in STEM @ UC San Diego does not discriminate, or grant preferences, on the basis of race, color, national origin, religion, sex, disability, and/or other protected categories. All enrolled students are encouraged to participate in oSTEM @ UC San Diego's events and activities.
